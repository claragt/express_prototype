Claim compensation for loss of notice

You may be able to claim compensation if you haven’t been paid for your loss of notice.

Use this service if you have:

  • claimed a redundancy payment
  • received a letter telling you to claim
  • a National Insurance number

If you don’t enter any information for more than 30 minutes, your claim will time out and you’ll need to start again.

Start now

Information that will help your claim

You will need to provide details if you have received one or both of the following during your notice period:

  • benefit payments
  • any type of earnings

You should previously have made a Claim a redundancy payment.
